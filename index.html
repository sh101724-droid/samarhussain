What is 99 nights in the forest?

99 nights in the forest is a game about... litterally what it sounds like. In this game, you are supposed to rescue people and uncover the secrets of the forest, all in 99 nights.


What are the objectives of 99 nights in the forest

The objectives of 99 nights in the forest are to find the 4 missing children, get your campfire to level 6 (which reveals the whole map) and protect your base from invaders, sent by the main antagionist, the deer.

The deer is one out of 5 of the antagonists of the game,

What are the entities abilities?

There are multiple different entities in 99 nights in the forest. The main one is the deer, as we said before. These entities are, the owl, the deer, the ram, the frog king, the bat
All these entities have abilities.
The owl's ability is that its the 2nd, fastest entity in the game and it causes you to stop moving.
The ram's ability is that it can ram into people and it's increased strength.
The Frog kings ability is that he can give you the poison effect by hitting you with his tongue. He is also the only entity who can be killed.
The bat's ability is that he can shriek and that makes you take about 25% damage. He can also summon shadow cultist amd a juggernaut shadow cultist.
The deer's ability is that the longer it chases you, the more speed and strength it gains.
It also gains more strength and speed as the days pass.
And finally, the deer can become hungry, which boosts its speed and hunger by a lot, making it the fastest entity in the game.
